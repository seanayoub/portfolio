Portfolio Work

Interactive Data Journalism | Digital Storytelling

I authored a data-driven piece relating to Climate Change and built an interactive webpage from the ground up to support its storytelling. To create UI components, I used Svelte [HTML | CSS | Javascript] and developed a time-series map feature as well as a scroll-based data visualization made with D3. The data used for the piece was obtained from the OpenMeteo Weather API. The piece itself is a survey of how increasing precipitation levels are affecting cities nationwide, with a focus on coastal cities in the United States. As this was my first time working with many of these tools, it was a challenge in the beginning to learn how to make interactive webpage elements. I'm excited at how sleek and professional the finished website turned out.

Final Analytics Report | Information Visualization

As part of a team, I completed an analysis on the housing market in California including its economic and geographic influences. The focal point of this project was the visualizations. We utilized a broad range of Python frameworks, including matplotlib, altair, geopandas, and D3 in Javascript. Several of the visualizations have interactive features, namely tooltips and radio buttons to filter selections. Using spatial data to create maps was a challenge and took a lot of trial and error to make work. However, it was helpful as we learned that a home's proximity to the ocean was a large factor in determining its price, especially in cities along the coast - San Diego, San Francisco, and Los Angeles. With more time, it would have been nice to examine various environmental and social factors, such as temperatures, healthcare, and safety.

Final Research Paper | Applied Econometrics

I conducted an independent statistical analysis on the American housing market to learn what factors influence rental prices. Data was obtained from the American Housing Survey. To complete this research, I utilized both Python and Stata to generate linear regression models and visualizations that clarify and enhance findings. I also included a discussion of relevant literature. The biggest challenge in completing this report was learning to clean and process the data so that the results of the regression models were accurate. In all, it seems the total number of rooms in a property has the most profound impact on price. To extend this research, I might study specific neighborhoods and incorporate spatial data into the analysis.

Natural Language Processing | Advanced Programming with Data

For this project, I utilized object-oriented programming in Python to build a reusable framework for text analysis and natural language processing. Specifically, I conducted research on speech in the context of humor. To do this, I obtained the scripts from ten stand-up comedy specials released on Netflix within the past few years in order to determine which features create the best comedy. The biggest factor influencing laughs was the subject the comedian was talking about - political satire ranked the lowest, especially compared to other topics like marriage and relationships. If I wanted to extend this project, I would use other metrics besides sentiment scores to draw new conclusions.

Final Database Implementation | Database Design

Through a group project, I created, designed, and implemented a database to make access to NBA basketball data more streamlined and reliable. Using principles of database design, we first constructed an entity-relationship diagram before drawing a true relation schema. We then replicated the database in SQL with sample data and wrote queries to illustrate the retrieval of information illustrated by the database. The final database allows users to track player's career progressions, compare team performances, and calculate advanced statistics, among others. As database design can be very particular, it was tricky making sure everything was done correctly and methodically. The finished product offers broad support for team analysts, sports journalists, and fans to access comprehensive NBA data.
